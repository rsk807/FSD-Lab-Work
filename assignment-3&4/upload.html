How Document Upload Works

Understanding the encryption and steganography process

1

File Selection

Choose your document from your device. We support various formats including PDF, Word documents, text files, and images.

2

AES-256 Encryption

Your document is encrypted using the industry-standard AES-256 encryption algorithm, ensuring maximum security.

3

Key Generation

A unique decryption key is generated and embedded into a stego-image using advanced steganography techniques.

4

Secure Storage

The encrypted document is stored on our secure servers, while the stego-image is provided to you for safekeeping.
