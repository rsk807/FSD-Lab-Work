Why Choose StegoCloud?

Decryption keys are hidden within innocent-looking images using advanced steganography techniques, making them virtually undetectable.

How StegoCloud Works

Simple steps to secure document sharing

1

Upload Your Document

Select your document and upload it to our secure servers. We support PDF, DOC, DOCX, TXT, and image files.

2

Automatic Encryption

Your document is encrypted with a unique key using AES-256 encryption. The key is then hidden in a stego-image.

3

Receive Secure Links

You receive a document link and a stego-image. Both are required to access your document - keeping it doubly secure.

4

Share & Access

Share both the link and stego-image with authorized recipients. They can decrypt and access the document securely.
